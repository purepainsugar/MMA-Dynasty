# WFC Lightweight Pilot Division

## Purpose

The WFC Lightweight division is the first test division for MMA Dynasty.

It will be used to build and test:

- fighter data
- rankings
- champions
- fight offers
- fight camps
- fight history
- records
- generated fighter depth
- world simulation

## Division Size

- Total Fighters: 35
- Hand-Crafted Fighters: 12
- Generated Fighters: 23

## Ranked Structure

- Champion: 1
- Ranked Fighters: 15
- Unranked Fighters: 20

## Hand-Crafted Fighter Slots

1. Current Champion
2. Former Champion
3. #1 Contender
4. Top-5 Knockout Artist
5. Top-5 Submission Specialist
6. Ranked Veteran Gatekeeper
7. Ranked Veteran Decision Fighter
8. Rising Prospect
9. Undefeated Prospect
10. Dangerous Unranked Fighter
11. Declining Former Name
12. Personality / Easter Egg Fighter

## Generated Fighter Pool

The remaining 23 fighters will be generated using:

- name
- age
- country
- record
- style
- archetype
- rating range
- potential range
- portrait seed

## Development Rule

Do not expand to other divisions until WFC Lightweight can support the full gameplay loop.

## Starting Promotion

- Promotion: World Fighting Championship
- Abbreviation: WFC
- Division: Lightweight
- Weight Limit: 155 lbs
- Ranking Size: Top 15
- Champion: TBD

## Design Notes

This division should feel alive before the rest of the world exists.

The first 12 hand-crafted fighters should create:

- title history
- veteran depth
- prospect excitement
- stylistic variety
- future rivalries
- enough personality to make the division memorable
